RankForgeAI Account Deletion
Use this form to request the complete deletion of your account and all associated personal data from our systems.

In accordance with Google Play's Data safety requirements, you may request the deletion of your account and data without needing to install the RankForgeAI application.

What happens when you delete your account?

  • Your profile and login credentials will be permanently removed.
  • All your mock test history, AI chat logs, and study progress will be wiped.
  • Your active premium subscription state will be detached. (Note: You must still cancel your recurring payment directly inside your Google Play Subscriptions menu to stop future charges).
  • This action is irreversible.

Once requested, our team will process the deletion and securely wipe your records within 7-14 business days.
